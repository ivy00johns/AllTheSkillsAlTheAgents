# The Hive

## Complete Naming System

The governing metaphor is tight: **a hive is a living system of distributed intelligence with shared memory, specialized roles, and a keeper who shapes conditions rather than doing the work.** Every name below lives inside that frame.

## The Killer Ones

If you had to pick five names that are genuinely unforgettable:

1. **The Comb** — for shared persistent memory. Perfect.
2. **The Smoker** — for the CLI. A beekeeper's smoker calms a hive and gives you access. That's exactly what a CLI does.
3. **The Glass** — for the dashboard. An observation hive has a glass wall. You look in without disturbing.
4. **The Keeper** — for the human-in-the-loop role. Not a manager, not a user. A keeper.
5. **The Waggle** — for the skill/tool registry. The waggle dance is how bees tell each other *where to go and what to do.* That is exactly what a skill registry does.

## The One-Sentence Frame

> *The Hive is a living orchestration system. Workers do the work. The Comb holds the memory. The Queen routes the swarm. The Glass lets you see inside. The Keeper decides when to harvest.*

That's your README opening. Everything else follows from it.
